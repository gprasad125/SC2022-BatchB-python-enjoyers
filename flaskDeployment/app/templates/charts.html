Charts

Scatter Matrix

This visualization shows correlation between multiple variables. This could be useful for determining groups of variables correlated with potability, which is important information to our classification process. Here we show the Seaborn sns.scatterplot to plot this Scatterplot Matrix that shows the relationship of the the column: Potability, with every other column. These results help inform our modeling process of the correlation between the potability and the all the other columns.

Histogram

This histogram displays the number of samples that were potable or not based on the level of conductivity, and is great in indicating obvious patterns between potability and other aspects of water. These results help inform our modeling process of the correlation between potability and other columns.


Violin Plot

This violin plot displays the spread of the data representing solids. This can also be used to display multiple groups of data for comparison. Furthermore, the plot shows the summary statistics of the data like a box-plot. This data can then be used to inform our classification process.

Heatmap

This heatmap basically shows how close two factors are related. The bottom row is the most important part, because it shows the relation of everything with potability. The darker the box is, the more correlated the two factors are.




Scatter Plot

The plot shows the correlation between the solids and hardness in the water sources from our dataset. Notably, no linear correlation exists between the two variables, indicative of the scatter matrix result from earlier.
