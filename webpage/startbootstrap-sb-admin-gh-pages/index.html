Water Quality

  1. Our goal is to predict the potability of water using classifying machine learning techniques. Using elements from our dataset we aim to classify new inputs as either potable (drinkable) or not potable (undrinkable).
  • Dataset column definitions:
    pH - The measure of acidity in the water.
    Hardness - The measure of heavy metals in the water.
    Solids - The measure of total dissolved solids in the water.
    Chloramines - The measure of disinfectants in the water.
    Sulfate - The measure of sulfate in the water.
    Conductivity - The measure of electrical conductivity in the water.
    Organic Carbon - The measure of carbon in the water.
    Trihalomethanes - The measure of water treatment in the water.
    Turbidity - The measure of clarity in the water.
    Potability - Whether the water is able to be consumed. (0 = Non potable, 1 = Potable)
